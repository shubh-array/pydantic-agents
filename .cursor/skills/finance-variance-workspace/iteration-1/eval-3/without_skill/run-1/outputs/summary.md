## Key Findings

- **3 of 5 categories came in over budget**, driving the overall overage.
- **Office, supplies** had the highest variance percentage at +26.15% (+$850.00), flagging potential for review despite the small absolute amount.
- **Marketing** contributed the largest absolute overage at +$2,500.00 (+20.00%).

- **Engineering** and **HR** were under budget by -$2,500.00 (-2.94%) and -$500.00 (-2.78%) respectively, partially offsetting overages elsewhere.

## Notes

- Variance is calculated as `actual − budget`; positive values indicate overspend.
- Variance % is calculated as `(actual − budget) / budget × 100`.
